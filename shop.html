The Shop

What I Make

Personalized apparel, made to order. Here's a look at the pieces I make most — and where prices start.

Something for every occasion

Made Just for You

Sweatshirts, crewnecks, hoodies, tees and keepsakes — personalized with names, appliqué, embroidery or print. Mix and match colors, fonts and fabrics until it's exactly yours.

Name & Family Appliqué

Mama, Mimi, Lola, Nauna, Gigi, Papa, Pawpaw — bold fabric-appliqué letters in gingham, floral or the print you pick.

Weddings & Couples

Bride & Groom, Hubby & Wifey, married-name sets and little sleeve dates to mark the day.

Baby & Little Ones

Coming-home outfits, name rompers, hearts and keepsakes for the newest arrivals.

Nurse & Profession

Titles, icons and designs for the work you're proud of — stitched by a nurse who gets it.

Names, Monograms & Dates

Delicate embroidered script names, monograms, hearts and dates — on their own or added to any piece.

DTF, Vinyl & Sewing

Full-color DTF prints, vinyl designs, split-name pieces and sewn projects like quilt-appliqué sweatshirts.
